Frequently asked questions

Have any inquiries? please feel free to search for a specific topic or explore the sections below before leaving a question

How Do I Cancel My Order? +
You can only cancel your order if it is not dispatched from our store.
In case you still wish to cancel the order, please contact our support team.

To cancel the order:
Login to your account > Go to Manage your Order > Cancel the desired order
Can I Change My Delivery Address? +
Yes, you can change your delivery address if the order is not yet dispatched from our store.
I received a wrong product, what should I do? +
Get in touch with our support team within 24 hours, along with some images & unboxing video of the incorrect product received. Our support team will resolve your issue as soon as possible.
How do I return the product? +
Unfortunately we do not accept return. We may only approve requests for returns only if the item has a manufacturing defect and cannot be replaced.
When will my order be shipped? +
Orders are typically shipped out within 24 to 48 hours.
How do I track my order? +
Once the order has been shipped out of our store, a whatsapp message will be sent to your phone no. with the tracking number.

Alternatively, you can track your order by:
Login to your account > Go to Manage your Order > Select the order > click on track
Where is my order? +
Once your product has been shipped, our logistics partner will handle the delivery. In case you are facing unexpected delays in delivery, you can always contact our support team for assistance.
I am not available to pickup my order? +
You can always give the product OTP for receiving the order to you neighbour to assist in receiving your order.

When will my refund be issued if I cancel my order?+
Refunds ussually take up to 7 days to be processed by the bank and credited back to your account.
Do I need to pay any additional taxes or fees? +
All prices on the website are inclusive of all taxes, and no additional amount will be charged to you. Some products may have additional shipping fees.
Cash on delivery is not available in my region? +
Unfortunately our logistic partner may not offer Cash on Delivery on your region, we are currently working to improve our services.

If you are experiencing problems with your product, you can fill out an RMA form and Get in touch with us so that we will assist you further.
